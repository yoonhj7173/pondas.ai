What is Craft?

Craft is a web app where a solo founder runs a virtual company of AI agents. Each project is an office map; teams are rooms; agents animate as they work.

🧑

Who is it for?

For solo founders who need planning, research, design, dev, and data done — but it’s just you. No game skills, no config files. If you can use Notion, you’re good.

⚡

Why is it different?

You wire agents together by dragging, not by writing YAML. And when the dev team says “done,” it means the app actually runs — tested for real, not just “build passed.”

How it works

1
Pick your teams
Choose from five templates. They appear as rooms with agents already at their desks.
2
Wire the graph
Connect agents with handoffs and review loops. The graph you draw is the graph that runs.
3
Talk to dispatch
Tell the orchestrator what you want in plain language. It routes work to the right teams.
4
Watch & steer
See status at a glance. Get notified when an agent finishes, blocks, or needs you.
